## Case Study 1: Aaron Kromer
**Realtor & Investor | Lehigh Valley, PA**

### The Situation
Aaron Kromer was running a real estate investment and sales business the way most operators do - manually. Follow-ups, paperwork, lead tracking, client communication. All of it eating hours every week that should have gone toward closing deals.

### What We Built
Working with PRISM AI Consultants, Aaron implemented a complete AI-assisted workflow covering:
- Automated follow-up sequences for leads at every stage of the pipeline
- AI-assisted paperwork drafting and document prep
- CRM integration with intelligent lead scoring
- Weekly reporting automation that replaced hours of manual tracking

### The Result
**Saving an estimated 50 hours per month on paperwork and follow-ups.**

That is 600 hours per year - the equivalent of 15 full work weeks - redirected from administrative tasks back into revenue-generating activity.
