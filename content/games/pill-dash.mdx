Pill Dash is an arcade-style pharmacy simulation game where speed meets accuracy. Rush through prescriptions, manage the queue, and keep customers satisfied without sacrificing safety.

Built by a real pharmacist who lived this chaos - and turned it into a game.

## How to Play

Navigate the pharmacy floor, fill prescriptions before the timer runs out, and avoid critical errors that tank your score. Each level increases the volume and complexity of the shift.

## Why It Exists

After Pharmageddon proved that pharmacy workers and patients both connect with this world, Pill Dash brings a faster, more arcade-focused take on the same universe.
