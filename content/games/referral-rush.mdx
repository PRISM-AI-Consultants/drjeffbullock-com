Referral Rush is a time-management networking game inspired by the BNI (Business Network International) referral system. Build genuine business relationships, give quality referrals, and grow your network - all while managing the clock and competing priorities.

## Gameplay

Each round represents a networking meeting. You must balance listening, connecting, following up, and delivering results. Give more to get more - but strategic relationship building beats random connections every time.

## Why This Game Exists

Dr. Bullock is an active BNI member and understands that referral-based business development is a system, not luck. This game captures the dynamics of networking done right - and shows why givers gain.
