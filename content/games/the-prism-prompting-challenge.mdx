The PRISM Prompting Challenge turns prompt craft into a puzzle. Each icon on the board represents a key part of a high-performing AI prompt. Match the right combinations to clear levels and sharpen the way you think about talking to AI.

## The Challenge

Clear each level by pairing the building blocks that belong together: the same combinations PRISM coaches clients through when they craft prompts to scale their business. It plays light, but it is practice for your AI brain.

## Why It Matters

The gap between average AI users and power users is almost entirely in how they communicate with the model. Knowing the components of a strong prompt, cold, is the first step.
