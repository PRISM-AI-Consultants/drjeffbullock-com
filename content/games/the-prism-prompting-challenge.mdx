The PRISM Prompting Challenge puts your AI communication skills to the test. Write prompts, evaluate outputs, refine your approach - and see how your score stacks up.

Prompt engineering is a skill. This game proves it.

## The Challenge

Each round gives you a goal and a blank prompt box. Write the most effective prompt to achieve the objective. The AI scores your output against the ideal result - and you learn exactly why one phrasing works better than another.

## Why It Matters

The gap between average AI users and power users is almost entirely in how they communicate with the model. This game closes that gap.
