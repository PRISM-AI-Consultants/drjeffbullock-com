Triple Threat: Retatrutide is a bullet-hell roguelike game built by a pharmacist who understands the science. Inspired by the triple-agonist mechanism of Retatrutide (GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon receptors), the gameplay mirrors the drug's multi-pathway approach to metabolic challenge.

## Gameplay

Navigate increasingly chaotic waves of metabolic threats. Each receptor pathway grants different abilities. Activate all three for devastating combo attacks - but managing resources across three systems is the real challenge.

## The Pharmacy Connection

Dr. Bullock's pharmacy background makes this more than a novelty. The game mechanics are grounded in real pharmacology, turning complex receptor science into intuitive gameplay that anyone can pick up but takes real skill to master.
