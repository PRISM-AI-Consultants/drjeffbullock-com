PRISM Sparks is an educational game built to make AI concepts click. Instead of reading documentation, you play through scenarios that challenge your understanding of how AI works in real business contexts.

Designed for entrepreneurs, professionals, and anyone who learns better by doing.

## What You'll Learn

- How AI models process and respond to different inputs
- Practical AI implementation concepts
- How to identify high-value AI use cases in business

## Built For

Business owners, executives, and operators who want to understand AI without wading through technical papers.
