District Zero drops you into an abandoned urban zone where resources are scarce and every decision matters. Scavenge for supplies, build shelter, and fight to reclaim territory from the forces that drove everyone out.

## How to Play

Navigate the district, collect resources, and make strategic choices about what to build and when to fight. Every run is different.
