Valley Growth turns economic development into a falling-block puzzle. Each piece is a sector of the Lehigh Valley economy: infrastructure, logistics, housing, retail. Place them well and the community thrives. Stack them sloppy and growth stalls.

## How to Play

Classic falling-block controls: move, rotate, soft drop, hard drop. Each sector piece comes with a quick note on what it contributes to a thriving economy, so you pick up a little regional economics while you chase the high score.

## The Partnership

Built in partnership with The Greater Lehigh Valley Chamber of Commerce and powered by PRISM AI.
