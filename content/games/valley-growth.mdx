Valley Growth is a business simulation game set in a stylized version of the Lehigh Valley economy. Start with a small operation, make smart decisions about people and technology, and scale before the market shifts.

## What You're Managing

- Hiring and team composition
- AI tool adoption and automation decisions
- Revenue growth vs operational cost
- Market timing and competitive positioning

## The Lesson

The game is a simulation of the real choices facing every small business owner right now. Do you invest in AI early or wait? Do you hire generalists or specialists? Every decision has a compounding effect.
