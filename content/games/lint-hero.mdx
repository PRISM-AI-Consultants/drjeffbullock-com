Lint Hero is a puzzle game built around the same discipline that separates good operators from great ones: catching the problem before it becomes someone else's problem.

Scan, identify, fix. Level up. Stay sharp.

## The Concept

Every level presents a workflow, document, or system with hidden errors. Your job is to find them all before time runs out. Miss too many and the system breaks. Find them all and you're the hero.

## The Philosophy

Built on the belief that quality control is underrated - and that the instinct to catch errors before they become disasters is a skill worth developing deliberately.
