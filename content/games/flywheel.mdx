Escape Velocity is an economic strategy game where you play as a Chapter Architect managing community money flow. Based on The Black Advantage by Dr. Jeff Bullock, the game challenges you to keep dollars circulating locally while building a self-sustaining economic ecosystem.

## How to Play

- **Direct Money Flow** - Keep financial resources circulating within the community rather than leaking out
- **Build Trust Networks** - Connect community nodes to reduce transaction friction and strengthen resilience
- **Launch Businesses** - Choose from 8 categories (grocery, restaurant, tech, health, finance, education, trade, retail) to capture spending
- **Train Workers** - Establish savings circles and hire trained workers to strengthen operations
- **Counter Extraction** - Defend against predatory lenders, gentrification, and big box displacement
- **Build Combos** - Chain 100+ transactions to reach "Escape Velocity" with massive multipliers

## Features

- Velocity Score system tracking money retention and combo chains
- Sprint mode (5-minute timed challenges) and Endless mode
- 3 difficulty levels: Easy, Normal, Hard
- 8 business categories with strategic network placement
- Dynamic music that crossfades based on gameplay intensity
- Extraction events with unique visual effects and counter mechanics
- Positive community events (rallies, grants, viral moments, block parties)
- Canvas 2D rendering at 60fps
- Single HTML file, zero dependencies, pure JavaScript

## The Thesis

Every mechanic maps to a real economic concept from The Black Advantage. The flywheel effect of circulating dollars, the damage of extraction, and the power of community trust networks. Your Velocity Score reflects how well you have built a self-sustaining local economy. Reach Escape Velocity and the community thrives on its own.

Built as a companion to The Black Advantage book and the Dollar Router financial framework at dollarrouter.app.
