Max the Flying Chicken is an educational platformer game based on the children's book by Layla Bullock. Guide Max through multiple levels - the Barnyard, the Pond, and the Festival - as he learns to fly and discovers what he's capable of.

## Features

- Multi-level game engine with physics and collision detection
- NPC dialog system with story progression
- Particle effects and smooth animations
- PWA-ready for offline play
- Designed for young players with simple, intuitive controls

## The Story

Max is a chicken who dreams of flying. Everyone tells him chickens can't fly. This game lets players prove them wrong - one level at a time.

Built as a companion piece to the illustrated children's book.
