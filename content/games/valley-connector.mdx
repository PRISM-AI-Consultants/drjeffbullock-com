Valley Connector is a strategy game inspired by the entrepreneurial ecosystem of the Lehigh Valley, PA. Build your network, connect resources to opportunities, and watch a community grow.

A love letter to the region where PRISM AI Consultants was built.

## Gameplay

Each round presents a map of businesses, talent, resources, and challenges. Your job is to make the right connections at the right time - turning isolated nodes into a thriving network.

## The Real Story

Built to celebrate the Lehigh Valley AI and entrepreneurship community. Every node in the game represents something real.
