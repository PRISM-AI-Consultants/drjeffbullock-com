Grid Guardian is a strategy defense game where you protect critical infrastructure from cascading failures. Build redundancy, respond to threats, and keep the system running under pressure.

## How It Works

Your grid starts stable. Then variables hit: demand spikes, node failures, bad actors, resource constraints. Your job is to anticipate, adapt, and keep the lights on.

## The Metaphor

Every business is a grid. Every entrepreneur who's managed systems under pressure knows this feeling. Grid Guardian turns that experience into a game - and teaches the systems thinking that makes operations resilient.
