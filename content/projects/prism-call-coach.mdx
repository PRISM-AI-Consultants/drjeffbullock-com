PRISM Call Coach listens to your live sales call and feeds you the next question, the objection answer, and the right offer in real time. It does not replace your preparation. It augments it with an expert in your ear.

## How It Works

Your browser captures the call audio, streams it to the coaching engine, and within seconds you get a private side panel that only you can see. It reads the conversation and surfaces:

- The next discovery question to go deeper
- A specific reframe the moment the prospect hesitates
- The right offer and positioning for who you are talking to
- A post-call brief with key moments, commitments, and follow-ups

## What Powers It

- **Live transcription and coaching** by Google Gemini, tuned on real sales frameworks
- **In-browser audio capture**, so there is nothing to install and it runs on any modern desktop
- **Deal-aware**, you can load a prospect brief so the guidance is specific to the call, not generic
- **Connected**, every call can feed the broader PRISM system for follow-up

## Use Case

Built for the conversations that decide whether a deal moves. New reps ramp faster, and experienced closers stop leaving questions unasked. The difference between a good call and a closed one is often one question at the right moment. Call Coach is there to catch it.
