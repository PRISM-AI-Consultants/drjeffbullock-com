PRISM University is an AI education platform hosted on Skool.com. It provides structured learning paths for business professionals who want to implement AI in their operations.

## What's Offered

- **Certification Tracks** - structured programs in AI implementation covering prompt engineering, automation, and AI-augmented workflows
- **Free Community Tier** - open access community with 500+ members for discussion, resource sharing, and peer learning
- **Weekly Live Content** - regular sessions including LinkedIn Live shows and community recaps
- **Practical Focus** - all content is oriented toward business application, not academic theory

## Philosophy

PRISM University is built on the principle that AI education should produce outcomes, not just knowledge. Every module is designed around practical application - what you learn this week, you use next week. The certification tracks are structured to build far transfer skills, not just tool-specific procedures.

## Community

The Skool platform enables community-driven learning alongside structured content. Members share wins, troubleshoot problems, and hold each other accountable. The free tier ensures accessibility while premium tracks provide deeper, hands-on implementation support.
