PRISM AI Consultants is an AI consulting firm founded by Dr. Jeff Bullock. The company helps businesses implement artificial intelligence for three measurable outcomes: revenue generated, time saved, and ease created.

## What We Do

PRISM works with business owners and leadership teams to integrate AI into their operations - not as a novelty, but as infrastructure. Services range from initial AI activation (getting teams comfortable with the tools) to full-scale partnerships where PRISM builds and manages AI systems for the business.

## Track Record

- 75+ leaders coached through AI adoption
- Custom AI agent systems built for client operations
- Meeting intelligence pipelines deployed in 30 minutes per client
- Active client engagements across consulting, legal, healthcare, and professional services

## Approach

PRISM's approach is operator-first. We don't sell AI education for its own sake. We identify where AI creates the most leverage in a specific business, build the system, train the team, and measure the outcome. Every engagement is tied to one of the three outcomes: did it make money, save time, or reduce friction?

The firm operates with its own AI agent system - 50 modules running autonomously for email triage, market intelligence, session recap, and strategic planning. We use the same tools we implement for clients.
