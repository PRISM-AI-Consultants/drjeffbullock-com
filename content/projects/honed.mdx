Honed is a cognitive training application designed to do what most brain training apps promise but fail to deliver: produce skills that transfer to real-world performance.

## The Problem

Most brain training apps improve your score on the app itself but produce no measurable improvement in general cognitive ability. The science calls this the "near transfer" problem - you get better at the game, not at thinking.

## The Approach

Honed is built on the research framework developed in Dr. Bullock's book Far Transfer. It uses evidence-based training protocols specifically designed to maximize far transfer - the ability to apply trained skills in entirely new contexts.

## Status

In active development. The research framework is established and the training protocol design is underway.

## Technical Stack

Built as a progressive web app with gamification mechanics inspired by Duolingo's engagement model but applied to cognitive science protocols.
