FliQ Runner (part of the WealthWise Kids initiative) is a 3D endless runner game that teaches financial literacy to children through fast-paced gameplay. Players run through environments collecting resources, avoiding financial pitfalls, and making split-second money decisions.

## The Mission

Financial literacy should start early and feel fun - not like homework. FliQ Runner turns budgeting, saving, and smart spending into reflex-based gameplay that kids actually want to play.

## Technical Details

- Built with Three.js and Vite for smooth 3D performance in the browser
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Progressive difficulty that introduces more complex financial concepts as players advance
- Score system tied to financial health metrics, not just speed

## Connection

FliQ Runner connects to Dr. Bullock's broader mission of economic empowerment. Teaching kids financial thinking early is a long-term play for generational wealth building.
