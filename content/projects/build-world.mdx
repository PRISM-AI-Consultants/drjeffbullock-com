Build World is the interactive companion to Agent World. While Agent World showcases the AI agent fleet, Build World showcases the full creative and intellectual output - every book, game, research framework, music project, and company built with AI as a force multiplier.

## What It Covers

- **10 books** across fiction, non-fiction, and educational categories
- **11 playable games** and interactive experiences
- **3 original research frameworks**
- **A 19-track concept album** (Mansa Musa)
- **2 companies** (PRISM AI Consultants and VersAssist)
- **A 54-module autonomous AI system** with 33 agents

## The Point

This is not a portfolio. It is proof of concept. One person, using AI as a force multiplier, producing at a scale that would normally require an entire team. Build World makes that output tangible and explorable.
