VersAssist is an AI-trained offshore labor company that makes scaling accessible for businesses that can't afford traditional domestic hiring costs.

## The Model

Like Uber democratized personal drivers, VersAssist democratizes having a competent, AI-capable team. We recruit offshore talent, train them on AI tools and workflows, and deploy them to businesses at rates that make growth economically viable.

The result: business owners who were stuck as solopreneurs can suddenly afford the team they need to grow. Operations, administration, content, outreach - the tasks that consume a founder's time can be handled by a trained team member at a fraction of domestic cost.

## Structure

VersAssist LLC is co-owned by Jeff Bullock (70%) and Mike Olaiya (30%). Jeff handles strategy, AI training methodology, and client acquisition. Mike handles operations and team management on the delivery side.

## Why It Matters

95% of Black business owners in America are solopreneurs. Not by choice - by economics. VersAssist directly addresses this by making labor accessible at rates that work for small business margins. When every business owner can afford a team, the ceiling on what they can build rises dramatically.

VersAssist operates alongside PRISM AI Consultants. PRISM provides the AI strategy and systems. VersAssist provides the people to run them.
