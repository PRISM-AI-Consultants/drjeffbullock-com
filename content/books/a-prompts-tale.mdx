A Prompt's Tale takes readers on a narrative journey through the fundamentals of artificial intelligence and prompt engineering. Rather than a dry technical manual, this book uses storytelling to make AI accessible to anyone - from complete beginners to business professionals looking to understand the technology shaping their industries.

## What You'll Learn

- The foundations of how AI language models work
- Practical prompt engineering techniques you can use immediately
- How to think about AI as a tool, not a replacement
- Real-world applications across business, creative, and personal contexts

## Who This Is For

Business owners, professionals, and curious minds who want to understand AI without the jargon. If you've ever wondered how to actually talk to AI and get useful results, this book is your starting point.

Written by Dr. Jeff Bullock, a pharmacist turned AI consultant who has coached 75+ leaders through AI adoption.
