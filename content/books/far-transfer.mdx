Far Transfer explores the science of how skills learned in one domain transfer to entirely different contexts. It draws on cognitive science, neuroscience, and learning theory to answer a practical question: can you train your brain to be better at everything?

## Status

In progress. Research phase and early drafting.

## Connection to Honed

This book provides the scientific foundation for Honed, Dr. Bullock's cognitive training application. The research frameworks developed here directly inform the app's training protocols and measurement systems.

## Themes

- Near transfer vs. far transfer in cognitive science
- The gap between lab results and real-world performance
- Designing training that actually generalizes
- Why most brain training apps fail - and what would work
