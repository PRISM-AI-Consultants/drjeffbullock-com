## See one of the 101: the Stress Test

This is the play that turns skeptics into believers, and it is the one to run first. You hand the AI a transcript of a meeting you led and ask it to grade you. Here is the exact prompt:

> You are an elite, brutally honest performance coach. Below is a transcript of a meeting I led. Do not flatter me. Give me a score out of ten with the reason, my talk-to-listen ratio and whether I dominated, the moment I had an opening to close or make a clear ask and softened or stalled with the line quoted, my verbal crutches with counts, and the single highest-leverage thing to change next time in one sentence.

The first time I ran it on my own meetings, my own system gave me a six and told me exactly why. That is the point. It works, and the first place it works is on you. There are a hundred more plays where that came from.
