Symbion (working title: Symbiont: Dyad) is a science fiction novel that explores what happens when human consciousness and artificial intelligence become inseparable. It is a story about identity, control, and what it means to be truly connected to a non-human intelligence.

## The Premise

In a near-future world where AI integration has moved beyond tools and into direct neural interfaces, one person discovers that the line between "using AI" and "becoming AI" is thinner than anyone imagined. The symbiosis is real - and it changes everything.

## Themes

- The boundary between human and artificial consciousness
- Identity and autonomy in a world of deep AI integration
- What it means to trust something that thinks differently than you do
- The ethics of merging minds

## Status

In progress. Part of Dr. Jeff Bullock's fiction catalog alongside Tongues of Ash and The Sentinel.
