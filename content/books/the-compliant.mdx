The Compliant explores the space between doing what you're told and losing who you are. Set in a world that rewards compliance and punishes deviation, the story follows someone who has always followed the rules - until the rules stop making sense.

## Themes

- Obedience vs. authenticity
- The systems that reward conformity
- The moment when compliance becomes surrender
- Finding your voice after years of silence
