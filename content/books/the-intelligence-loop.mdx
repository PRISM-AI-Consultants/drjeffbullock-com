The Intelligence Loop is a 44,503-word non-fiction book presenting a unified framework for continuous improvement. Currently in v4.

## Core Concept

The Intelligence Loop connects three domains that are usually treated separately: learning science (how people actually retain and transfer knowledge), AI tools (how to use them as thinking partners rather than answer machines), and coaching methodology (how to structure accountability and growth over time).

## Connection to Practice

The framework is not theoretical. It emerges directly from PRISM's coaching practice, where the loop runs live with real clients every week. Session transcripts feed analysis. Analysis feeds strategy. Strategy feeds the next session. The book documents the system that makes this cycle repeatable.

## Audience

Business owners, coaches, consultants, and anyone building a practice around helping others improve. The book is written for practitioners who want a system, not a philosophy.
