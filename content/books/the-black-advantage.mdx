The Black Advantage is an evidence-based framework for economic empowerment in the African American community. It connects historical context to modern tools - AI, community finance, cooperative economics, and strategic acquisition - to lay out a one-generation path from disadvantage to abundance.

## The Framework

Built around the Abundance Flywheel and organized into 4 Parts with 14 chapters. Every chapter opens with a real story. Each includes Builder's Checklists with 6-7 actionable items.

## Key Components

- **The Abundance Flywheel** - self-reinforcing economic engine
- **Dollar Router** - fintech tool for community-directed spending
- **Digital Susu** - modernized rotating savings circles
- **Kill-Switch Governance** - community accountability mechanisms
- **Silver Tsunami Acquisition** - strategic business succession planning
- **Diaspora Direct Investment** - global Black economic connection
