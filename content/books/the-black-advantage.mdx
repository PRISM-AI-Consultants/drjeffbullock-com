The Black Advantage is an evidence-based non-fiction book laying out actionable strategies for economic empowerment in the African American community. It connects historical context to modern tools - including AI, entrepreneurship, and financial systems.

## Status

First full draft complete. In editorial review.

## Themes

- Generational wealth building
- Technology as an equalizer
- Community-scale economic strategy
- From historical disadvantage to strategic advantage

## Connection to the Mission

This book is a direct expression of one of Dr. Bullock's core goals: solving poverty starting with African Americans. It provides the intellectual framework that projects like the Bullock Economic Resilience Index quantify.
