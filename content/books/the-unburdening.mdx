The Unburdening is a novella about the invisible weight people carry - obligations, expectations, grief, and the stories we tell ourselves about who we're supposed to be.

Through a series of interconnected moments, the narrative explores what happens when someone decides to put the weight down. Not dramatically. Not all at once. But deliberately, with the quiet courage it takes to choose yourself.

## Themes

- The cost of carrying what isn't yours
- Identity beyond obligation
- Small acts of freedom
- The difference between giving up and letting go
