Prompt Power is the sequel to A Prompt's Tale - the book that started it all. Where A Prompt's Tale introduced beginners to AI and prompt engineering, Prompt Power takes readers to the next level with advanced techniques, multi-step workflows, and real-world case studies from coaching hundreds of business leaders.

## What This Book Covers

- Advanced prompt engineering patterns and frameworks
- Multi-step AI workflows for complex business tasks
- Real case studies from PRISM AI consulting engagements
- How to chain prompts for research, writing, and analysis
- Building repeatable AI-powered processes that compound over time

## Who It Is For

Business leaders, entrepreneurs, and professionals who already understand AI basics and want to master it. If A Prompt's Tale was the introduction, Prompt Power is the masterclass.
