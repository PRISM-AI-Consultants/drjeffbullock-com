The Inheritance examines what families pass down - not just property or money, but patterns, beliefs, unfinished business, and unspoken expectations.

This short story follows a character confronting an inheritance that is more burden than gift, and the decision of what to carry forward and what to leave behind.

## Themes

- Generational patterns
- The weight of family expectations
- Choosing your own path
- What we owe the past vs. what we owe ourselves
